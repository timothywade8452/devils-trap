DEVIL'S TRAP

A first-person rage-bait maze. The floor is a liar.

One real exit

Reach the glowing orb. The doors carved into the walls are decorative — they go nowhere. Chasing them is how you die.

No tells

Pits, spikes, launch pads and crushers are painted to look exactly like safe floor. You cannot dodge the first one. That's the point.

Beatable by memory

Traps never move. Every spot that kills you gets scorched red on your next run, so the maze that's impossible at first becomes a route you simply remember.

The ten floors

  • The Threshold — just walk to the light. (You won't make it.)
  • Decorative Doors — the exits in the walls are bait.
  • The Floor Lies — solid-looking tiles drop into the dark.
  • Pillars of Salt — black pillars split the room; hidden pits fill the gaps.
  • Hot Floor — lava puddles you can see, traps you can't.
  • Launch Window — one tile flings you somewhere fatal.
  • Overhead — the ceiling has opinions.
  • The Long Con — the safe path doubles back on itself.
  • No Tells — everything you learned, weaponized.
  • The Floor Is Lava — literally. It rises. Run.

Controls

Desktop: WASD or arrows to move · mouse to look · space to jump · R to restart the floor · esc to free the cursor.
Mobile: left thumbstick to move · drag the right side to look · JUMP button.

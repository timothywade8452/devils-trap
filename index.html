DEVIL'S TRAP

A first-person rage-bait maze. The floor is a liar.

One real exit

Reach the glowing orb. The doors carved into the walls are decorative — they go nowhere. Chasing them is how you die.

No tells

Pits, spikes, launch pads and crushers are painted to look exactly like safe floor. You cannot dodge the first one. That's the point.

Beatable by memory

Traps never move. A shaft of light marks the one true exit — learn the safe path that reaches it and the maze that's impossible at first becomes a route you simply remember.

Thirty floors · three descents

  • Act I — The Threshold (cold slate) — learn the lie. Doors, pits, spikes, lava, launches, crushers.
  • Act II — Salted Earth (rust & amber) — bigger rooms, denser traps, the floor starts to rise.
  • Act III — No Mercy (toxic violet) — everything you learned, weaponized twice, all the way to the Devil's Door.

⚔ Arena campaign — 50 levels

A full first-person boss-rush, designed from deep research into what makes arena shooters and rage hits explode. Fire glowing energy bubbles at hovering, multi-phase bosses and a swarm of 10 distinct enemy types; dash to dodge, circle the cover, and outlast the lie.

  • World 1 — The Proving Floor (cyan) — learn the lie; meet the Overseer.
  • World 2 — Ember Reach (amber) — fire & lava; the Forgemaster floods the floor.
  • World 3 — The Drowned Vault (teal) — dark, fake-safe ground; the Drowned Choir.
  • World 4 — Stormspire (violet) — shrinking arenas; the Tempest Crown.
  • World 5 — The Liar's Throne (crimson) — everything lies; face the Devil.

A boss every 10th level, rotating objectives (slay · survive · hold · blitz · hunt · duel · gauntlet · sudden-death), stacking modifiers (armored / swift / frenzy …) and hazards (🔥 lava · 🌑 dark · ⭕ shrinking arena · 🌙 low-gravity · 💀 one-hit). Pick any unlocked level from the grid — or take on ☠ The Grind, an endless Cyber-Grind of escalating waves scored by kills. A troll narrator taunts every death.

Settings & mobile

Full in-game settings (⚙) — volume, brightness, bloom, quality, move speed, look sensitivity, field of view, and more. 100% mobile: on-screen joystick or D-pad, jump / sprint / fire, with adjustable opacity, size, handedness, and drag-to-reposition controls.

🛒 Souls & the Soul Shop

Every floor you clear, boss you drop and arena you win pays Souls — more on Brutal, with a bonus for no-death runs. Spend them in the Soul Shop on skins (recolour your energy-bubbles, sonar, dash trail & crosshair — 8 of them, up to the legendary Void Walker) and fair upgrades (Soul Magnet ×2, Scout's Reserve +2 pings, Quick Step, Death Echo, Iron Heart) — none of which trivialise the rage-bait core. It all saves locally.

Sonar, difficulty & dash

Out of your depth? Spend a limited sonar ping (F) to flash the traps near you — enough to de-risk a step, never enough to map the maze. Choose your pain in Settings: Casual (5 pings, slow lava), Normal, or Brutal (no pings, no exit beacon, fast lava). A live timer tracks your best run per floor. In the Arena, dash to dodge boss volleys and grab health dropped by drones.

Controls

Desktop: WASD move · shift sprint · mouse look · space jump · F sonar · double-tap to dash · scroll to zoom · R restart · M mute · esc to free the cursor.
Mobile: joystick to move · drag to look · JUMP / RUN / PING buttons · in Arena, FIRE auto-aims and DASH dodges.

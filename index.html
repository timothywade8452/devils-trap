DEVIL'S TRAP

A first-person rage-bait maze. The floor is a liar.

One real exit

Reach the glowing orb. The doors carved into the walls are decorative — they go nowhere. Chasing them is how you die.

No tells

Pits, spikes, launch pads and crushers are painted to look exactly like safe floor. You cannot dodge the first one. That's the point.

Beatable by memory

Traps never move. A shaft of light marks the one true exit — learn the safe path that reaches it and the maze that's impossible at first becomes a route you simply remember.

Thirty floors · three descents

  • Act I — The Threshold (cold slate) — learn the lie. Doors, pits, spikes, lava, launches, crushers.
  • Act II — Salted Earth (rust & amber) — bigger rooms, denser traps, the floor starts to rise.
  • Act III — No Mercy (toxic violet) — everything you learned, weaponized twice, all the way to the Devil's Door.

⚔ Arena mode

Step out of the maze into a vast open hellfield. Three bosses hover and rotate far across the arena, repositioning and raining projectile spreads while ground drones swarm you. Fire back with glowing energy bubbles that streak across the field. Dodge, circle the cover pillars, and drop all three before they drop you.

Settings & mobile

Full in-game settings (⚙) — volume, brightness, bloom, quality, move speed, look sensitivity, field of view, and more. 100% mobile: on-screen joystick or D-pad, jump / sprint / fire, with adjustable opacity, size, handedness, and drag-to-reposition controls.

Controls

Desktop: WASD or arrows to move · shift to sprint · mouse to look · space to jump · scroll or + − to zoom · R to restart the floor · M to mute music · esc to free the cursor.
Mobile: left thumbstick to move · drag the right side to look · JUMP button.

Built with vanilla JS + Three.js. No build step, no dependencies bundled. · Part of the Devil's series.
